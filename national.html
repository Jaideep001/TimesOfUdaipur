Deputy CM Fadnavis Accorded Grand Welcome in Hometown Nagpur; Shah's Photo Missing from BJP Banners



Maharashtra Deputy Chief Minister Devendra Fadnavis on Tuesday slammed the previous Maha Vikas Aghadi (MVA) government, saying it had no connect with common citizens as he arrived in his hometown Nagpur on his first visit after assuming charge of his new office and was accorded a grand welcome by BJP workers. However, what caught everyone's attention was the absence of Union Home Minister and senior BJP leader Amit Shah's photo from the banners and hoardings up by party workers to welcome Fadnavis, who took over as Deputy CM in the Eknath Shindeled government on June 30.
